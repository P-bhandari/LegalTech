Central Government Act

Section 3 in The All- India Institute Of Medical Sciences (Amendment) Act,   2002

 3.  Substitution of new section for section 24.- For section 24 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:-" 24. Grant of medical, dental or nursing degrees, diplomas, etc., by the Institute.- Notwithstanding anything contained in any other law for the time being in force, the Institute shall have power to grant medical, dental or nursing degrees, diplomas and ther academic distinctions and titles under this Act.". SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
