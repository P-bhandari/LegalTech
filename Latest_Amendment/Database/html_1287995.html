Central Government Act
Section 22(2) in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act, 2002
(2) A general meeting held in pursuance of sub- section (1) shall, subject to any directions of the Central Government, be deemed to be an annual general meeting of the company: Provided that in the case of revival and rehabilitation of sick industrial companies under Chapter VIA, the provisions of this section shall have effect as if for the words" Central Government", the word" Tribunal" had been substituted.'.
