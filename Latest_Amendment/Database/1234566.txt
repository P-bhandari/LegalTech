Central Government Act

Section 12D(2) in The National Commission For Minority Educational Institutions (Amendment) Act,   2006

 (2)  The Commission may, for the purpose of conducting any investigation pertaining to a complaint under this Act, utilise the services of any officer of the Central Government or any State Government with the concurrence of the Central Government or the State Government, as the case may be. (3) For the purpose of investigation under sub- section (1), the officer whose services are utilised may, subject to the direction and control of the Commission,- (a)  summon and enforce the attendance of any person and examine him;  (b)  require the discovery and production of any document; and  (c)  requisition any public record or copy thereof from any office.
