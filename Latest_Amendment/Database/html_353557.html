Central Government Act
Section 20(b) in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act, 2002
(b) that on other grounds, it is just and equitable to grant relief, may on the application of the company or any person interested and on such terms and conditions as it may seem to the Central Government just and expedient, direct that the time for the filing of the particulars or for the registration of the charge or f r the giving of intimation of payment or satisfaction shall be extended or, as the case may require, that the omission or mis- statement shall be rectified.
(2) The Central Government may make such order as to the costs of an application under sub- section (1) as it thinks fit.
(3) Where the Central Government extends the time for the registration of a charge, the order shall not prejudice any rights acquired in respect of the property concerned before the charge is actually registered.".
