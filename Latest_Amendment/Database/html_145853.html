Central Government Act
Section 26(a) in The Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances (Amendment) Act, 2001
(a) for sub- section (1), the following sub- section shall be substituted, namely:-" (1) Whenever any offence punishable under this Act has been committed, the narcotic drug, psychotropic substance, controlled substance, opium poppy, coca plant, cannabis plant, materials, apparatus and utensils in respect of which or by means of which such offence has been committed, shall be liable to confiscation.";
