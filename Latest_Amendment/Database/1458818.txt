Central Government Act

Section 10FZ in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act,   2002

 10FZ.  Protection of action taken in good faith.- No suit, prosecution or other legal proceedings shall lie against the Appellate Tribunal or its Chairperson, Member, officer or other employee or against the Tribunal, its President, Member, officer or oth r employee or operating agency or liquidator or any other person authorised by the Appellate Tribunal or the Tribunal in the discharge of any function under this Act for any loss or damage caused or likely to be caused by any act which is in good faith d ne or intended to be done in pursuance of this Act.
