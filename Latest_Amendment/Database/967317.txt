Central Government Act

Section 7 in The Trade Unions (Amendment) Act,   2001

 7.  Amendment of section 11.- In section 11 of the principal Act, in sub- section 1, after clause (a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- '' (aa) where the head office is situated in an area, falling within the jurisdiction of a Labour Court or an Industrial Tribunal, to that Court or Tribunal, as the case may be;''.
