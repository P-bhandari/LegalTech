Central Government Act

Section 6 in The Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Amendment Act,   2004

 6.  Substitution of new section for section 10.- For section 10 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:-" 10. Penalty for being member of an unlawful association, etc.- Where an association is declared unlawful by a notification issued under section 3 which has become effective under sub- section (3) of that section,- (a)  a person, who- (i)  is and continues to be a member of such association; or  (ii)  takes part in meetings of such association; or  (iii)  contributes to, or receives or solicits any contribution for the purpose of, such association; or  (iv)  in any way assists the operations of such association, shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to two years, and shall also be liable to fine; and   (b)  a person, who is or continues to be a member of such association, or voluntarily does an act aiding or promoting in any manner the objects of such association and in either case is in possession of any unlicensed firearms, ammunition, explosive or other instrument or substance capable of causing mass destruction and commits any act resulting in loss of human life or grievous injury to any person or causes significant damage to any property,- (i)  and if such act has resulted in the death of any person, shall be punishable with death or imprisonment for life, and shall also be liable to fine;  (ii)  in any other case, shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than five years but which may extend to imprisonment for life, and shall also be liable to fine.".
