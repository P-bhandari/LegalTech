Section 1 in The Criminal Law (Amendment) Act,   2005

 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Criminal Law (Amendment) Act,  2005 .  (2)  It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint; and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of this Act.
