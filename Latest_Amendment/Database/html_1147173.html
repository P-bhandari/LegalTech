Central Government Act
Section 2 in The National Tax Tribunal (Amendment) Act, 2007
2. Amendment of section 5.- In section 5 of the National Tax Tribunal Act, 2005 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub- section (5),-
(i) the words" in consultation with the Chairperson" shall be omitted;
(ii) the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:-" Provided that no Member shall be transferred without the concurrence of the Chairperson.".
