Central Government Act
Section 15 in The Cost And Works Accountants (Amendment) Act, 2006
15. Amendment of section 17.- In section 17 of the principal Act,-
(a) in sub- section (1), for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:-" (ii) a Finance Committee; and";
(b) for sub- section (3), the following sub- section shall be substituted, namely:-" (3) Each of the Standing Committees shall consist of the President and the Vice- President ex officio, and minimum of three and maximum of five members to be elected by the Council from amongst its members .";
(c) sub- sections (4) and (5) shall be omitted;
(d) in sub- section (6), for the words" two- thirds of total membership of the Committee", the words" one- third of total membership of the Committee" shall be substituted.
