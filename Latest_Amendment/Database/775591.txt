Central Government Act

The Aircraft (Amendment) Act,   2000

























 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Aircraft (Amendment) Act,  2000 .  (2)  It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
