Central Government Act
Section 10FO in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act, 2002
10FO. Delegation of powers.- The Tribunal may, by general or special order, delegate, subject to such conditions and limitations, if any, as may be specified in the order, to any Member or officer or other employee of the Tribunal or other person authoris d by the Tribunal to manage any industrial company or industrial undertaking or any operating agency, such powers and duties under this Act as it may deem necessary.
