Central Government Act
Section 26 in The Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001
26. Applicability of order passed.- An order passed by the Authority under this Chapter shall be binding on the assessing authorities and other authorities created by or under any law relating to general sales tax, in force for the time being in any State or Union territory.'. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
