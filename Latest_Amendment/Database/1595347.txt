Central Government Act

Section 12(9) in The Merchant Shipping (Amendment) Act,   2002

 (9)  If a survey has been completed and a new certificate cannot be issued or placed on board the ship before the expiry date of the existing certificate, the Central Government or any person authorised by it in this behalf may endorse the existing certi icate and such certificate shall be in force for a further period which shall not exceed five months from the expiry date of the existing certificate.
