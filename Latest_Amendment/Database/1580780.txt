Central Government Act

Section 1 in The Enforcement Of Security Interest And Recovery Of Debts Laws (Amendment) Act,   2004

 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Enforcement of Security Interest and Recovery of Debts Laws (Amendment) Act,  2004 .  (2)  Save as otherwise provided in this Act, the provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th day of November,  2004 .
