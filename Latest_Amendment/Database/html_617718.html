Central Government Act
Section 9 in The Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 2001
9. Repeal and saving.-
(1) The Taxation Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001 (Ord. 2 of 2001 ) is hereby repealed.
(2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of this Act. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
