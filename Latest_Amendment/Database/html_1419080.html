Central Government Act
Section 1 in The Deposit Insurance Corporation (Amendment And Miscellaneousprovisions) Act, 1978
1. Short title and commencement.
(1) This Act may be called the Deposit Insurance Corporation (Amendment and Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, 1978 .
(2) It shall come into force on such date 1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint, and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of this Act; and any reference in any provision to the commencement of this Act shall be construed as a reference to the commencement of that provision.
