Central Government Act
Section 424C(1) in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act, 2002
(1) If after making an inquiry under section 424B, the Tribunal is satisfied that a company has become a sick industrial company, the Tribunal shall, after considering all the rele ant facts and circumstances of the case, decide, as soon as may be, by an order in writing, whether it is practicable for the company to make its net worth exceed the accumulated losses or make the repayment of its debts referred to in clause (b) of sub- ection (2) of section 424A within a reasonable time.
