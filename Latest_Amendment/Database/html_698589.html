Central Government Act
Section 3 in The Transfer Of Property (Amendment) Act, 2002
3. Transitory provisions.- The provisions of section 106 of the principal Act, as amended by section 2, shall apply to-
(a) all notices in pursuance of which any suit or proceeding is pending at the commencement of this Act; and
(b) all notices which have been issued before the commencement of this Act but where no suit or proceeding has been filed before such commencement. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
