Central Government Act
Section 31 in The Code Of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act, 2005
31. Amendment of section 377.- In section 377 of the principal Act,-
(a) in sub- sections (1) and (2), for the words" an appeal to the High Court against the sentence on the ground of its inadequacy", the following shall be substituted, namely:-" an appeal against the sentence on the ground of its inadequacy-
(a) to the Court of Session, if the sentence is passed by the Magistrate; and
(b) to the High Court, if the sentence is passed by any other Court"; (b) in sub- section (3), for the words" the High Court", the words" the Court of Session or, as the case may be, the High Court" shall be substituted.
