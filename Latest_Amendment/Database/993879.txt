Central Government Act

Section 15 in The Insurance (Amendment) Act,   2002

 15.  Amendment of section 101A.- In section 101A of the principal Act, in sub- section (8), for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:-' (ii)" Indian re- insurer" means an Indian insurance company which has been granted a certificate of registration under sub- section (2A) of section 3 by the Authority to carry on exclusively the re- insurance business in India.'.
