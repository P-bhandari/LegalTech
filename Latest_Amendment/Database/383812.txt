Central Government Act

Section 10FZA(4) in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act,   2002

 (4)  All proceedings before the Tribunal or the Appellate Tribunal shall be deemed to be judicial proceedings within the meaning of sections 193 and 228, and for the purposes of section 196, of the Indian Penal Code (45 of  1860  ) and the Tribunal and the A pellate Tribunal shall be deemed to be a civil court for the purposes of section 195 and Chapter XXVI of the Code of Criminal Procedure,  1973  (2 of  1974  ).
