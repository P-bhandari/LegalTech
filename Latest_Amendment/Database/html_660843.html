Central Government Act
Section 424A(1) in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act, 2002
(1) Where an industrial company, has become a sick industrial company, the Board of directors of such company shall make a reference to the Tribunal, and prepare a scheme of its revival and rehabilitation and submit the same o the Tribunal along with an application containing such particulars as may be prescribed, for determination of the measures which may be adopted with respect to such company: Provided that nothing contained in this sub- section shall apply to a Government company: Provided further that a Government company may, with the prior approval of the Central Government or a State Government, as the case may be, make a reference to the Tribunal in accordance with the provisions of this sub- section and thereafter all the pro isions of this Act shall apply to such Government company.
