Central Government Act

Section 12(2) in The Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances (Amendment) Act,   2001

 (2)  Where the person referred to in sub- section (1) is liable to be punished with a minimum term of imprisonment and to a minimum amount of fine, the minimum punishment for such person shall be one- half of the minimum term of imprisonment and one- half of the minimum amount of fine: Provided that the court may, for reasons to be recorded in the judgment, impose a fine exceeding the fine for which a person is liable.
