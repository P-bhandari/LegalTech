Section 1 in The Merchant Shipping (Amendment) Act,   2002

 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Merchant Shipping (Amendment) Act,  2002 .  (2)  It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of this Act.
