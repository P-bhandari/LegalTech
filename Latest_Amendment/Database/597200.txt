Central Government Act

Section 1 in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act,   2002

 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Companies (Second Amendment) Act,  2002 .  (2)  It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint: Provided that different dates may be appointed for different provisions of this Act and any reference in any such provision to the commencement of this Act shall be construed as a reference to the commencement of that provision.
