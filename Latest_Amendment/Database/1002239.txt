Central Government Act

Section 28 in The Code Of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act,   2005

 28.  Amendment of section 320.- In section 320 of the principal Act, in the Table under sub- section (2),- (a)  the words" Voluntarily causing hurt by dangerous weapons or means" in column 1 and the entries relating thereto in columns 2 and 3 shall be omitted;  (b)  in column 3, for the word" Ditto", against the entry relating to section 325, the words" The person to whom the hurt is caused" shall be substituted;  (c)  in column 1, for the words" two hundred and fifty rupees", wherever they occur, the words" two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
