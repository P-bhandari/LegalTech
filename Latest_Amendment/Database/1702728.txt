Central Government Act

The Scheduled Castes And Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act,   2002

























 1.  Short title.- This Act may be called the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act,  2002 .

1.

 2.  Definitions.- In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,- (a) " Scheduled Castes Order" means the Constitution (Scheduled Castes) Order,  1950 , made by the President under article 341 of the Constitution;  (b) " Scheduled Tribes Orders" means the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order,  1950 , the Constitution (Uttar Pradesh) Scheduled Tribes Order,  1967  and the Constitution (Sikkim) Scheduled Tribes Order,  1978 , made by the President under article 342 of the C nstitution.
