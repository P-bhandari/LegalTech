Central Government Act

Section 3 in The Trade Unions (Amendment) Act,   2001

 3.  Amendment of section 5.- In section 5 of the principal Act, in sub- section 1, after clause (a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- '' (aa) in the case of a Trade Union of workmen, the names, occupations and addresses of the place of work of the members of the Trade Union making the application;''.
