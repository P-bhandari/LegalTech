Central Government Act

Section 10FS(1) in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act,   2002

 (1)  In the event of the occurrence of any vacancy in the office of the Chairperson of the Appellate Tribunal by reason of his death, resignation or otherwise, the senior- most Member of the Appellate Tribunal shal act as the Chairperson of the Appellate Tribunal until the date on which a new Chairperson appointed in accordance with the provisions of this Act to fill such vacancy enters upon his office.
