Central Government Act

Section 7 in The Administrative Tribunals (Amendment) Act,   2006

 7.  Substitution of new section for section 8.- For section 8 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:-" 8. Term of office.- (1) The Chairman shall hold office as such for a term of five years from the date on which he enters upon his office: Provided that no Chairman shall hold office as such after he has attained the age of sixty- eight years. (2)  A Member shall hold office as such for a term of five years from the date on which he enters upon his office extendable by one more term of five years: Provided that no Member shall hold office as such after he has attained the age of sixty- five years.  (3)  The conditions of service of Chairman and Members shall be the same as applicable to Judges of the High Court.".
