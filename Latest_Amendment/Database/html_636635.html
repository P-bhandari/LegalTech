Central Government Act
Section 9 in The Enforcement Of Security Interest And Recovery Of Debts Laws (Amendment) Act, 2004
9. Amendment of section 15.- In section 15 of the principal Act, in sub- section 1, for the words" When the management of business of a borrower is taken over by a secured creditor", the words, brackets, letters and figures" When the management of business of a borrower is taken over by a securitisation company or reconstruction company under clause (a) of section 9 or, as the case may be, by a secured creditor under clause (b) of sub- section (4) of section 13" shall be substituted.
