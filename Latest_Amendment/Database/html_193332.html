Central Government Act
Section 3(b) in The Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 2005
(b) after clause (iiid) as so inserted, the following clause shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day of April, 2001 , namely:-" (iiie) any profit on the transfer of the Duty Free Replenishment Certificate, being the Duty Remission Scheme under the export and import policy formulated and announced under section 5 of the Foreign Trade (Development and Regulation) Act, 1992 (22 of 2992);".
