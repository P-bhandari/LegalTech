Central Government Act

Section 35(b) in The Code Of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act,   2005

 (b)  after the first proviso, the following Explanation shall be inserted, namely:-" Explanation.- Where a person is unable to give bail within a week of the date of his arrest, it shall be a sufficient ground for the officer or the Court to presume that he is an indigent person for the purposes of this proviso.".
