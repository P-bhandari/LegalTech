Central Government Act
Section 36AAB(c) in The Banking Regulation (Amendment) And Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 2004
(c) an order for the supersession of the Board and the appointment of an Administrator therefore has been made under section 36AAA, such order for sanctioning the scheme of compromise and arrangement or reorganization or reconstruction under clause (a) or the winding up of the multi- State co- operative bank under clause (b) or an order for the supersession of the Board and the appointment of an Administrator under clause (c) shall not be liable to be called in question in any manner.
