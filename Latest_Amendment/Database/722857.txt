Section 10FW in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act,   2002

 10FW.  Salary, allow- ances and other terms and conditions of service of Chairperson and Members.- (1)  The salary and allowances and other terms and conditions of service of the Chairperson and other Members of the Appellate Tribunal shall be such as may be prescribed.  (2)  The salary, allowances and other terms and conditions of service of the Chairperson and other Members of the Appellate Tribunal shall not be varied to their disadvantage after appointment.
