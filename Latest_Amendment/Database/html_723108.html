Central Government Act
Section 17(e)(ii)(2) in The Merchant Shipping (Amendment) Act, 2002
(2) Claims set out in sub- section (1) shall be subject to limitation of liability even if brought by way of recourse or for indemnity under a contract or otherwise: Provided that claims set out in clause (d) of sub- section (1) shall not be subject to limitation of liability to the extent that they relate to remuneration under a contract with the person liable.
