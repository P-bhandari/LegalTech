Central Government Act
Section 4 in The Election Laws (Amendment) Act, 2003
4. Amendment of section 62.- In section 62 of the principal Act, after sub- section (5), the following sub- section shall be inserted, namely:-" (6) Nothing contained in sub- sections (3) and (4) shall apply to a person who has been authorised to vote as proxy for an elector under this Act in so far as he votes as a proxy for such elector.".
