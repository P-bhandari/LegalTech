Central Government Act

Section 2 in The Deposit Insurance Corporation (Amendment And Miscellaneousprovisions) Act,   1978

 2.  Definitions. In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,-- (a) " Company" means the Credit Guarantee Corporation of India Limited, a Company formed and registered under the Companies Act,  1956  (1 of  1956  ), and having its registered office at Bombay;  (b) " Corporation" means the Deposit Insurance Corporation established under sub- section (1) of section 3 of the Deposit Insurance Corporation Act,  1961  (47 of  1961  );  (c) " re- named Corporation" means the Corporation as re- named in accordance with the provisions of section 4;  (d)  words and expressions used in this Act and not defined but defined in the Deposit Insurance Corporation Act,  1961  (47 of  1961  ), shall have the meanings respectively assigned to them in that Act.
