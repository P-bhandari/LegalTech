Central Government Act
Section 2 in The Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances (Amendment) Act, 2001
2. Amendment of section 1.- In the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985 (61 of 1985 ) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 1, in sub- section (2), after the words" whole of India", the following shall be inserted, namely:-" and it applies also-
(a) to all citizens of India outside India;
(b) to all persons on ships and aircrafts registered in India, wherever they may be".
