Central Government Act

Section 3 in The Enforcement Of Security Interest And Recovery Of Debts Laws (Amendment) Act,   2004

 3.  Amendment of section 3.- In section 3 of the principal Act, in sub- section (3), after clause (g), the following clause shall be inserted at the end, namely:-" (h) that securitisation company or reconstruction company has complied with one or more conditions specified in the guidelines issued by the Reserve Bank for the said purpose.".
