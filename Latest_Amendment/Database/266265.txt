Section 1 in The Code Of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act,   2005

 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act,  2005 .  (2)  Save as otherwise provided in this Act, it shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
