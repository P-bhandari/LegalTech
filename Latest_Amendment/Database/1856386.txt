Section 2(c) in The Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act,   2005

 (c)  after sub- section (4), the following sub- section shall be inserted, namely:-" (4A) The directors appointed under clause (ff) of sub- section (1) shall be entitled to receive such salary and allowances as the Central Warehousing Corporation may, with the approval of the Central Government, determine.".
