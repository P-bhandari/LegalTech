Section 2 in The Scheduled Castes And Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act,   1956

 2.  Definitions. In this Act,-- (a) " article" means an article of the Constitution;  (b) " census authority" means the Deputy Registrar General, India;  (c) " last census" means the census held in  1951 ;  (d) " prescribed" means prescribed by rules made under this Act.
