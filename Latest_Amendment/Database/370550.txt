Section 2 in The Indian Medicine Central Council (Amendment) Act,   2002

 2.  Insertion of new Chapter IIA.- After Chapter II of the Indian Medicine Central Council Act,  1970  (48 of  1970  ) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following Chapter shall be inserted, namely:-' CHAPTER IIA PERMISSION FOR NEW MEDICAL INSTITUTION, COURSE, ETC.
