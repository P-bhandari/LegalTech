Section 20 in The Consumer Protection (Amendment) Act,   2002

 20.  Substitution of new sections for section 22.- For section 22 of the principal Act, the following sections shall be substituted, namely:-" 22. Power and procedure applicable to the National Commission.- (1) The provisions of sections 12, 13 and 14 and the rules made thereunder for the disposal of complaints by the District Forum shall, with such modifications as may be considered necessary y the Commission, be applicable to the disposal of disputes by the National Commission. (2)  Without prejudice to the provisions contained in sub- section (1), the National Commission shall have the power to review any order made by it, when there is an error apparent on the face of record.
