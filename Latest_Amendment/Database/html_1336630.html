Central Government Act
Section 25 in The Patents (Amendment) Act, 2005
25. Amendment of section 28.- In section 28 of the principal Act,-
(a) for sub- section (4), the following sub- section shall be substituted, namely:-" (4) A request or claim under the foregoing provisions of this section shall be made before the grant of patent.";
(b) sub- section (5) shall be omitted;
(c) in sub- section (6), for the words, brackets and figure" Subject to the provisions of sub- section (5), where", the word" Where" shall be substituted.
