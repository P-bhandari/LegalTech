Central Government Act

Section 15(ii) in The Citizenship (Amendment) Act,   2003

 (ii)  in sub- section (3), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:-" Provided that any rule made in respect of a matter specified in clause (ia) of sub- section (2) may provide that a breach thereof shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three months, or with fine which may extend to five thousand rupees, or with both.".
