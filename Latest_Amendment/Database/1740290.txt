Central Government Act

Section 38K in The Wild Life (Protection) Amendment Act,   2006

 38K.  Definitions.- In this Chapter,- (a) " National Tiger Conservation Authority" means the Tiger Conservation Authority constituted under section 38L;  (b) " Steering Committee" means the Committee constituted under section 38U;  (c) " Tiger Conservation Foundation" means the foundation established under section 38X;  (d) " tiger reserve State" means a State having tiger reserve;  (e) " tiger reserve" means the areas notified as such under section 38V.
