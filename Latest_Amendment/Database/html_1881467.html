Central Government Act
Section 2 in The Railways (Amendment) Act, 2005
2. Amendment of section 2.- In section 2 of the Railways Act, 1989 (24 of 1989 ) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),-
(a) after clause (1), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-' (1A)" Authority" means the Rail Land Development Authority constituted under section 4A;';
(b) after clause (32), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-' (32A)" railway land" means any land in which a Government railway has any right, title or interest;'.
