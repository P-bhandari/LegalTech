Central Government Act

Section 7 in The Merchant Shipping (Amendment) Act,   2002

 7.  Substitution of new sub- heading for sub- heading above section 299.- For the sub- heading" Safety certificates, safety equipment certificates, safety radio telegraphy certificates, safety radio telephony certificates, exemption certificates, etc." above ection 299 of the principal Act, the following sub- heading shall be substituted, namely:-" Safety certificates, safety equipment certificates, safety radio certificates, exemption certificates, etc.".
