Central Government Act

Section 10B(2) in The Cost And Works Accountants (Amendment) Act,   2006

 (2)  A person shall not be qualified for appointment,- (a)  as a Presiding Officer of the Tribunal unless he has been a member of the Indian Legal Service and has held a post in Grade I of the service for at least three years;  (b)  as a Member unless he has been a member of the Council for at least one full term, and who is not a sitting member of the Council or who has not been a candidate in the election under dispute; or  (c)  as a Member unless he holds the post of a Joint Secretary to the Government of India or any other post under the Central Government carrying a scale of pay which is not less than that of a Joint Secretary to the Government of India.
