Central Government Act
Section 10FE in The Companies (Second Amendment) Act, 2002
10FE. Term of office of President and Members.- The President and every other Member of the Tribunal shall hold office as such for a term of three years from the date on which he enters upon his office, but shall be eligible for re- appointment: Provided that no President or other Member shall hold office as such after he has attained,-
(a) in the case of the President, the age of sixty- seven years;
(b) in the case of any other Member, the age of sixty- five years: Provided further that the President or other Member may retain his lien with his parent cadre or Ministry or Department, as the case may be, while holding office as such.
