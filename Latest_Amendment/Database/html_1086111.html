Central Government Act
Section 3 in The Anti- Corruption Laws (Amendment) Act, 1967
3.
(1) Repeal and saving. The Anti- Corruption Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 1967 (3 of 1967 ) is hereby repealed.
(2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
