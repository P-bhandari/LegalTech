Central Government Act

Section 17 in The Enforcement Of Security Interest And Recovery Of Debts Laws (Amendment) Act,   2004

 17.  Amendment of section 31.- In section 31 of the principal Act, in clause (g), for the words" any properties not liable to attachment", the words and brackets" any properties not liable to attachment (excluding the properties specifically charged with the debt recoverable under this Act)" shall be substituted.
