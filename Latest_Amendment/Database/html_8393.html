Central Government Act
Section 2 in The Punjab Municipal Corporation Law (Extension To Chandigarh) Amendment Act, 2000
2. Amendment of Act 45 of 1994 .- In the Punjab Municipal Corporation Law (Extension to Chandigarh) Act, 1994 , in Part II of the Schedule, in section 4, in sub- section (3),-
(a) in clause (ii), for the words" municipal administration.", the words" municipal administration; and" shall be substituted;
(b) after clause (ii), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-" (iii) the member of the House of the People representing the constituency which comprises wholly or partly, the municipal area, with the right to vote.". SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
