Central Government Act

Section 1 in The Companies (Amendment) Act,   2000

 1.  Short title and commencement.- (1)  This Act may be called the Companies (Amendment) Act,  2000 .  (2)  The provisions of this Act, other than sections 7 and 80, shall come into force at once and sections 7 and 80 shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
