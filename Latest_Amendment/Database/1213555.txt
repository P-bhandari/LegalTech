Section 6(2) in The State Financial Corporations (Amendment) Act,   2000

 (2)  The option referred to in sub- section (1) shall be given by the Financial Corporation to every existing shareholder before the expiry of three months from the commencement of the State Financial Corporations (Amendment) Act,  2000  and shall be exercis d by the shareholder within three months from the date of receipt of such option.
