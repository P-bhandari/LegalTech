Central Government Act

Section 22 in The Merchant Shipping (Amendment) Act,   2002

 22.  Insertion of new section 352FA.- After section 352F of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely:-" 352FA. Power to make rules.- The Central Government may make rules to carry out the purposes of this Part: Provided that the rules under this Part shall be made having regard to the provisions of the Convention.".
