Central Government Act
Section 16(5) in The Code Of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act, 2005
(5) The State Government may, subject to such control and directions as it may deem fit to impose, by general or special order, delegate its powers under sub- section (4) to the District Magistrate. Explanation.- The word" arms" shall have the meaning assigned to it in section 153AA of the Indian Penal Code (45 of 1860 ).'.
