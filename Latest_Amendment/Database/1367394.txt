Central Government Act

Section 2 in The Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act,   2005

 2.  Amendment of section 7.- In section 7 of the Warehousing Corporations Act,  1962  (58 of  1962  ) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (a)  in sub- section (1),- (i)  clause (b) shall be omitted;  (ii)  after clause (f), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-" (ff) three directors to be appointed by the Central Government;";   (b)  in sub- section (4), the words" and a Vice- Chairman" shall be omitted;  (c)  after sub- section (4), the following sub- section shall be inserted, namely:-" (4A) The directors appointed under clause (ff) of sub- section (1) shall be entitled to receive such salary and allowances as the Central Warehousing Corporation may, with the approval of the Central Government, determine.".
