Central Government Act
Section 15B(2) in The Cost And Works Accountants (Amendment) Act, 2006
(2) The Universities or bodies referred to in sub- section (1) shall, while awarding degree, diploma or certificate or bestowing any designation, ensure that the award or designation do not resemble or is not identical to one awarded by the Institute. (3) Nothing contained in this section shall enable a University or a body to adopt a name or nomenclature which is in any way similar to that of the Institute.".
