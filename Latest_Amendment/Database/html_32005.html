Central Government Act
Section 6(1) in The State Financial Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2000
(1) On the commencement of the State Financial Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2000 , every shareholder shall be given by the Financial Corporation an option to require the Financial Corporation to convert the shares held by him into shares of the same n minal value without the State Government guarantee and issue fresh share certificate or to pay the amount paid in respect of such shares not exceeding the face value of the shares held by him.
