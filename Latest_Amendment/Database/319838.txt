Section 22 in The Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act,   2001

 22.  Powers of the Authority.- (1)  The Authority shall have the same powers as are vested in a court under the Code of Civil Procedure,  1908  (5 of  1908  ), while trying a suit in respect of the following matters, namely:- (a)  enforcing the attendance of any person, examining him on oath or affirmation;  (b)  compelling the production of accounts and documents;  (c)  issuing commission for the examination of witnesses;  (d)  the reception of evidence on affidavits;  (e)  any other matter which may be prescribed.
