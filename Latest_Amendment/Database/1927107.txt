Central Government Act

Section 2 in The Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act,   2005

 2.  Amendment of section 19.- In the Central Sales Tax Act,  1956  (74 of  1956  ) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 19, after sub- section (2), the following sub- section shall be inserted, namely:-" (2A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub- section (2), the Chairman or a Member holding a post as such in the Authority for Advance Rulings appointed under clause (a) or clause (c), as the case may be, of sub- section (2) of section 245-O of the Income- tax Act,  1961  (43 of  1961  ) may, in addition to his being the Chairman or a Member of that Authority, be appointed as the Chairman or a Member, as the case may be, of the Authority under this Act.".
