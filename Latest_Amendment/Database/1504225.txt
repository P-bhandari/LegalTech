Central Government Act

Section 38U(2) in The Wild Life (Protection) Amendment Act,   2006

 (2)  The Steering Committee shall consists of- (a)  the Chief Minister- Chairperson;  (b)  the Minister in- charge of Wild Life- Vice- Chairperson;  (c)  such number of official members not exceeding five including at least two Field Directors of tiger reserve or Director of National Park and one from the State Government' s Departments dealing with tribal affairs;  (d)  three experts or professionals having qualifications and experience in conservation of wild life of which at least one shall be from the field of tribal development;  (e)  two members from the State' s Tribal Advisory Council;  (f)  one representative each from State Government' s Departments dealing with Panchayati Raj and Social Justice and Empowerment;  (g)  Chief Wild Life Warden of the State shall be the Member- Secretary, ex officio, to be notified by the State Government, in the Official Gazette.
