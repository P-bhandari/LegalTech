Central Government Act

The Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities (Amendment) Act,   1996

























 1.  Short title. This Act may be called the Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities (Amendment) Act,  1996 .
